Admission Process

At Carebridge Training Institute, we believe in providing accessible education to all motivated individuals. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and supportive.

Step 1: Choose Your Program

Review our programs and select the one that best fits your career goals.

Step 2: Submit Application

Fill out our online application form with your personal and educational information.

Step 3: Document Submission

Provide required documents such as ID, certificates, and proof of payment.

Step 4: Interview (if required)

Some programs may require a brief interview to assess your motivation and fit.

Step 5: Enrollment

Once accepted, complete enrollment and attend orientation.

Admission Requirements

General Requirements:

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) or equivalent
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Good command of English
  • Valid ID or passport

Program-Specific Requirements:

  • Healthcare Programs: Biology background preferred
  • IT Courses: Basic math skills required
  • Business Programs: No specific prerequisites

Tuition and Fees

Program Duration Tuition Fee (KES)
Healthcare Training 6-12 months 45,000 - 75,000
IT Courses 3-9 months 30,000 - 60,000

Fees include tuition, materials, and certification. Payment plans available.

Scholarships and Financial Aid

We offer various scholarships and financial aid options to make education accessible:

  • Merit-based scholarships for high-achieving students
  • Need-based financial aid
  • Women in STEM scholarships
  • Employer-sponsored training programs
  • Flexible payment plans
