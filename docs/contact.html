Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions page or contact our admissions office directly.

What are the payment options?

We offer flexible payment plans, scholarships, and accept various payment methods including M-Pesa.

Do you offer online classes?

Currently, most of our programs are in-person, but we offer some blended learning options. Contact us for details.

How long do the programs take?

Program duration varies from 3-12 months depending on the course. Check individual program pages for details.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated career services team that helps graduates find employment opportunities.
