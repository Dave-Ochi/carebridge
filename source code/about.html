Our Story

Founded in 2010, Carebridge Training Institute has been at the forefront of vocational education in Kenya. We recognized the gap between traditional education and the practical skills needed in today's job market. Our institute was established to bridge this gap, providing hands-on training that directly translates to career success.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical skills for career success by offering high-quality, industry-relevant training programs in healthcare, information technology, and business administration.

Our Vision

To be the leading vocational training institute in East Africa, known for producing skilled professionals who drive innovation and economic growth.
